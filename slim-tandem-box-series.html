Product Overview

The Slim Tandem Box Series represents the pinnacle of drawer system engineering. This comprehensive collection features drawer side-boards ranging from 86mm to 300mm height with customizable width options. Each unit incorporates automatic error adjustment technology and hidden full pullout silencing skids capable of bearing 45kg loads.

The series features newly-designed built-in dampers that eliminate the need for reinstallation while ensuring soft close and smooth drawer movements. Perfect for premium kitchen installations, luxury furniture applications, and commercial-grade storage solutions.
